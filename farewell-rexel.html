Dear Team,

After a fulfilling six-year journey on the Rexel project, I have been presented with new opportunity that requires me to embark on a different path.

My tenure with the Rexel project has been instrumental in my professional growth. I began my journey with the run team, transitioned to the defects team, and soon after, I joined the build team. At that time, joining the build team was considered a rare and significant opportunity. I am deeply grateful to Mahesh S and Hemant C for their trust and for giving me this chance. Over the course of more than 100 sprints of active development and approximately 20-25 sprints as a tech lead, I have thoroughly enjoyed my time on this project.

When I joined this project, I was solely focused on technical aspects. Now, as I depart, I take with me valuable management skills learned from observing and working with our leadership. My experience at Rexel has been enriching due to the incredible people I have worked with. While it would be unfair to single out just a few, I must acknowledge the tremendous support from my colleagues in the development, QA and devops teams, many of whom moved on before the COVID pandemic.
